## English

2022 matters because artificial intelligence becomes newly visible to ordinary people in a direct and experiential way.

Earlier AI had already shaped many systems from behind the interface: recommendation, ranking, recognition, moderation, prediction, and optimization. But in 2022, another threshold is crossed. AI no longer appears only as hidden infrastructure. It begins to present itself directly through writing, image generation, conversation, and creative imitation.

This matters because the public does not merely hear that AI is improving. The public begins to feel it. A person can type a prompt and see text appear. A person can describe an image and watch it materialize. A person can converse with a system and encounter fluency where earlier generations of software would have collapsed.

The significance of this year lies in the change of social imagination. AI is no longer discussed only as a strategic field, a research frontier, or a background capability inside platforms. It becomes a public experience. The question changes from “Will AI matter?” to “What happens now that AI is already doing things people can immediately perceive?”

In China, as elsewhere, this matters because once AI becomes directly demonstrable, it rapidly enters everyday conversation, education, product design, media coverage, business planning, and public anxiety. The cultural distance between laboratory progress and mass awareness collapses.

Historically, 2022 is important because it marks the year when AI stops being mostly ambient and becomes theatrically present. The machine no longer merely sorts the world behind the curtain. It begins to speak, draw, draft, and surprise in public.

One-sentence summary:

> **The key to 2022 is that generative AI becomes publicly and experientially visible, turning AI from hidden capability into direct social fact.**

## 中文

2022 年的重要性，在于人工智能第一次以一种普通人可以直接感知、直接体验的方式，重新进入公共生活。

更早之前，AI 当然已经在界面背后深度作用于很多系统：推荐、排序、识别、审核、预测、优化，这些都离不开它。但到了 2022 年，一个新的门槛被跨过去了。AI 不再只是隐藏基础设施，它开始直接以写作、图像生成、对话和模仿创作的形式出现在人面前。

这件事重要，因为公众不再只是“听说 AI 进步了”，而开始真正“感受到 AI 在做事”。人可以输入一句提示词，看见一段文字生成出来；可以描述一个画面，看见图像被生成；可以和一个系统对话，并第一次遇到一种让此前软件显得迟钝的流畅性。

2022 年的意义，在于社会想象的变化。AI 不再只作为战略领域、科研前沿或平台内部能力被讨论，它开始变成一种公共经验。问题也从“AI 会不会重要”变成“既然 AI 已经开始当场做事了，我们要怎么理解接下来的变化？”

在中国，这一点同样关键，因为一旦 AI 变成可演示、可传播、可直接体验的东西，它就会迅速进入日常谈话、教育话语、产品设计、媒体报道、商业规划和公众焦虑之中。实验室进展和大众意识之间的距离，被一下子拉得非常近。

从历史上看，2022 年的重要性，在于它标记了 AI 从“环境中的能力”转向“舞台上的存在”。机器不再只是在幕后默默排序，它开始在台前说话、绘图、起草、模仿，并让人产生直接震动。

如果说 2021 年写的是云上工作场和远程常态如何沉淀，那么 2022 年写的就是：在这样的数字工作与生活基础上，AI 第一次大规模作为“可见合作者候选”进入普通人的眼前。它还没有完全稳定地嵌入所有工作流，但它已经先改变了所有人对于“机器能不能参与创造和表达”的判断。

一句话概括：

> **2022 年的关键，是生成式 AI 以可体验、可传播、可直接感知的形式进入公众生活，使 AI 从隐藏能力变成了公开的社会事实。**
