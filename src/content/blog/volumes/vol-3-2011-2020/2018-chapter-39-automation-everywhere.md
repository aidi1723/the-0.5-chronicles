## English

2018 matters because automation becomes less visible as a special system and more pervasive as a hidden condition of ordinary life.

Automation had long been associated with factories, industrial machinery, and large-scale production systems. But by 2018, automation is increasingly present in places that do not look industrial at all: office workflows, customer service, logistics coordination, recommendation refreshes, moderation pipelines, household devices, and background software operations.

This matters because the meaning of automation changes. It is no longer mainly understood as machines replacing manual labor in a dramatic, physical setting. Instead, automation becomes the quiet replacement, acceleration, or pre-structuring of countless small steps. Forms auto-fill, messages auto-sort, systems auto-respond, services auto-route, and platforms auto-decide what proceeds next.

The significance of this year lies in the deepening invisibility of machine mediation. People increasingly live inside processes that are partially automated without always noticing where the handoff occurs. Human choice still exists, but the steps leading up to choice are more frequently prearranged.

In China, this matters because digital platforms, smart devices, logistics systems, and large-scale service infrastructures all create fertile ground for automation to enter daily life. What earlier seemed like separate innovations now begin to converge into a more generalized condition: ordinary action increasingly passes through automated steps.

Historically, 2018 is important because it broadens automation from a production concept into a civilizational condition. Automation is no longer just about making goods faster. It is also about shaping transactions, communication, management, and domestic routine through machine-handled transitions.

One-sentence summary:

> **The key to 2018 is that automation sinks beneath everyday life, turning more and more ordinary steps into machine-mediated processes.**

## 中文

2018 年的重要性，在于自动化开始越来越不像一个特殊系统，而越来越像普通生活背后的一种基础条件。

在很长时间里，自动化更多让人联想到工厂、工业机械和大规模生产线。但到了 2018 年，自动化越来越多地出现在那些看上去并不“工业”的地方：办公室流程、客服系统、物流协调、推荐刷新、内容审核、家庭设备、后台软件运行。它不再只属于生产现场，而开始深入服务、管理、消费和家庭生活。

这件事重要，因为“自动化”的含义发生了变化。它不再主要被理解成机器在一个戏剧性的物理场景里取代人工，而越来越表现为：无数小步骤被悄悄替代、加速、预先安排。表单自动填充、消息自动分类、系统自动回复、服务自动分流、平台自动决定下一步该发生什么。

2018 年的意义，在于机器中介变得更隐蔽也更普遍。人当然还在做决定，但在很多时候，决定之前的路径、选项、顺序和节奏，已经先被系统安排好了。自动化不一定把人完全拿掉，却越来越多地重写人做事的前提。

在中国，这种变化尤其关键，因为平台体系、智能设备、物流网络和大规模服务基础设施的结合，为自动化进入日常生活提供了非常肥沃的土壤。此前看上去分散的创新，开始汇合成一种更普遍的状态：普通人的行动，越来越多地经过自动化步骤。

从历史上看，2018 年的重要性，在于它把自动化从“生产概念”扩展为一种“文明条件”。自动化不再只意味着更快地制造物品，它也意味着交易、沟通、管理和家庭日常，越来越多地经过机器处理的转换环节。

如果说 2017 年写的是界面如何从屏幕延伸到语言，那么 2018 年写的就是：在这些新的界面和平台背后，越来越多过程本身也开始被机器无声接管。自动化不再只是一个显眼工程，而开始成为生活的地基之一。

一句话概括：

> **2018 年的关键，是自动化开始沉入日常生活底部，让越来越多普通步骤变成机器中介的流程。**
