## English

2019 matters because perception infrastructure becomes harder to separate from ordinary space.

Cameras, sensors, smart devices, and connected hardware had already been spreading for years. But by 2019, their social meaning becomes sharper. These are no longer isolated gadgets. They increasingly form a distributed layer through which homes, streets, stores, vehicles, workplaces, and platforms can continuously observe, register, and react.

This matters because digital systems are no longer limited to what users explicitly type, upload, or click. More and more of the world begins to enter computation through passive capture. Vision, location, movement, proximity, temperature, entry, exit, and environmental change all become potential inputs.

The significance of this year lies in the widening of machine perception. Systems do not merely wait for commands. They increasingly monitor conditions. A camera can identify, a door sensor can report, a speaker can listen, a vehicle can transmit, and an edge device can process without sending every step to a distant center.

In China, this matters because urban infrastructure, consumer hardware, logistics, security systems, and platform ecosystems all contribute to the spread of a continuously sensed environment. The visible world becomes more deeply entangled with real-time registration and response.

Historically, 2019 is important because it marks a further transition from connected systems to perceptive systems. The network does not only link nodes; it increasingly notices the conditions of those nodes. Daily life becomes more trackable not only through self-report, but through ambient capture.

One-sentence summary:

> **The key to 2019 is that sensors, cameras, and edge devices make ordinary space increasingly machine-readable, turning perception into infrastructure.**

## 中文

2019 年的重要性，在于感知基础设施越来越难和普通空间分开。

摄像头、传感器、智能设备和联网硬件当然早已扩散多年，但到 2019 年，它们的社会含义变得更加尖锐：这些东西不再只是分散的电子小物件，而越来越像一层分布式的感知层。家庭、街道、商店、车辆、工作场所和平台，都可以通过这层感知层持续地观察、记录、反馈和响应。

这件事重要，因为数字系统不再只依赖用户主动输入、上传或点击。世界本身开始越来越多地通过“被动采集”进入计算。视觉、位置、移动、接近、温度、进出和环境变化，都开始成为潜在输入。

2019 年的意义，在于机器感知的进一步扩展。系统不再只是等人发出命令，而越来越多地处于“持续监听条件”的状态：摄像头可以识别，门磁可以上报，音箱可以监听，车辆可以传输，边缘设备甚至可以在本地完成部分处理，而不必把每一步都送往远端中心。

在中国，这一点尤其重要，因为城市基础设施、消费级硬件、物流网络、安全系统和平台生态共同推动了“持续感知环境”的扩张。可见世界越来越深地卷入实时记录和即时响应之中。

从历史上看，2019 年的重要性，在于它标记了一个从“连接系统”走向“感知系统”的进一步转折。网络不再只是连接节点，它开始越来越多地感知节点所处的状态。日常生活变得更可追踪，不只是因为人主动报告自己，也因为环境本身在不断被读取。

如果说 2018 年写的是自动化如何沉入日常流程，那么 2019 年写的就是：自动化赖以成立的另一部分基础，正在变得更加普遍——那就是持续、分布式、低感知度的机器感知。系统不只是更会处理事情，也更会“看见”事情。

一句话概括：

> **2019 年的关键，是传感器、摄像头与边缘设备让普通空间越来越可被机器读取，感知因此开始成为基础设施。**
