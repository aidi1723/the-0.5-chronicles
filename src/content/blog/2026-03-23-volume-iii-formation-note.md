## English

Today, **Volume III** of **The 0.5 Chronicles** can be formally marked as having taken shape.

Its title is:

> **The Platform Age (2011–2020)**

This volume follows the decade in which the internet no longer merely entered daily life, but increasingly organized daily life. Platforms, mobile systems, recommendation logic, data collection, digital payment, cloud services, automation, and machine perception moved from scattered functions into a more coherent social order.

### What this volume now contains

The line now established includes the following years and themes:

- 2011 — payment becomes a mobile habit
- 2012 — messaging becomes an ecosystem gateway
- 2013 — data becomes the exhaust of daily life
- 2014 — recommendation systems organize attention
- 2015 — deep learning makes AI newly believable at public scale
- 2016 — services become composable through APIs
- 2017 — voice becomes an everyday interface
- 2018 — automation sinks into ordinary routine
- 2019 — sensors, cameras, and edge devices turn perception into infrastructure
- 2020 — remote coordination and platform dependence scale into necessity

### Why this volume matters

If Volume II recorded the years when the internet entered ordinary life, Volume III records the years when platforms began to organize ordinary life from within.

In this decade, the user no longer simply goes online. The user increasingly lives inside platform logic: paying, communicating, being recommended to, being measured, being coordinated, being routed, and being continuously mediated.

This is why the period matters historically. It is not only about better software or more connected devices. It is about the emergence of a social order in which platforms become the default machinery of everyday continuity.

### What this formation note means

This note marks another structural step in the project.

2011–2020 is now recognized as a full volume line with its own title, scope, entry page, and continuous chapter sequence. It is no longer just an outline or a future plan. It has become an established part of the book's public architecture.

In one sentence:

> **On March 23, 2026, Volume III of The 0.5 Chronicles, *The Platform Age (2011–2020)*, formally took shape.**

## 中文

今天，《地球文明0.5编年史》**第三卷**可以正式标记为已经成形。

它的卷名是：

> **《平台时代》（2011—2020）**

这一卷写的是互联网不再只是进入普通生活，而开始越来越深地组织普通生活。平台、移动系统、推荐逻辑、数据收集、数字支付、云服务、自动化和机器感知，从分散功能逐步汇合成一种更连贯的社会秩序。

### 这一卷现在包含什么

目前已经建立起来的主线，包括以下年份与主题：

- 2011 —— 支付成为移动习惯
- 2012 —— 通讯界面成为生态入口
- 2013 —— 数据成为日常生活的尾气
- 2014 —— 推荐系统开始组织注意力
- 2015 —— 深度学习让 AI 在公共尺度上重新变得可信
- 2016 —— 服务越来越多地通过 API 被调用和拼装
- 2017 —— 语音成为日常界面
- 2018 —— 自动化沉入普通生活流程
- 2019 —— 传感器、摄像头与边缘设备让感知成为基础设施
- 2020 —— 远程协作与平台依赖从方便升级为必要

### 为什么这一卷重要

如果说第二卷记录的是互联网进入普通生活的过程，那么第三卷记录的，就是平台如何从普通生活内部开始组织普通生活。

在这十年里，用户不再只是“上网”，而越来越多地生活在平台逻辑之中：支付、沟通、被推荐、被测量、被协调、被分流、被持续中介。平台不再只是提供工具，而开始成为维持日常连续性的默认机器。

这也是这一时期在历史上真正重要的地方。它不只是软件更好、设备更多、连接更快，而是平台逐步成为普通社会运转机制的一部分。

### 这份成形说明意味着什么

这份说明标记的是项目结构上的又一步成形。

2011—2020 这一段，现在已经被正式承认为完整的一卷：它有自己的卷名、自己的范围、自己的卷页面入口，也有了连续的章节主线。它不再只是提纲，不再只是未来计划，而已经成为这本书公开结构中的稳定部分。

一句记录：

> **2026 年 3 月 23 日，《地球文明0.5编年史》第三卷《平台时代》（2011—2020）正式成形。**
