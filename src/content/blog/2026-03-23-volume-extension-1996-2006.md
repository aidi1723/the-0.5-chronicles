## English

Today, **The 0.5 Chronicles** added a continuous chapter run covering **1996–2006**.

This addition does not cancel the earlier completion note for Volume I. Instead, it extends the project’s visible yearly line forward and makes the transition from early internet entry to normalized online life much more continuous.

### What was added

The newly completed run includes the following yearly chapters:

- 1996 — *Modems and the Sound of Connection*
- 1997 — *Email and the First Inbox*
- 1998 — *Search as a New Sense*
- 1999 — *Cybercafes and Shared Screens*
- 2000 — *The Web as a Marketplace*
- 2001 — *Digital Cameras and Cheap Memory*
- 2002 — *Instant Messaging*
- 2003 — *Forums and Usernames*
- 2004 — *Broadband Quietly Arrives*
- 2005 — *The Feed*
- 2006 — *Tags and Folk Taxonomies*

### Why this matters

This run is important because it fills in a historically decisive transition.

If the earlier volume work established the formation of digital civilization before it became ordinary, this newly added sequence shows how the internet moved from a technical threshold into a lived environment:

- connection became experience,
- the inbox became a room of digital life,
- search became orientation,
- cybercafes democratized access,
- the web acquired commercial behavior,
- memory became digital,
- instant messaging made presence visible,
- forums thickened online identity,
- broadband turned access into background,
- feeds reorganized attention,
- and user participation began helping organize the web itself.

### What this note means

This is a project-extension note.

It marks a new stage in which the historical line no longer stops at the earlier closure point, but begins to extend more clearly into the years when the internet became ordinary social infrastructure.

In one sentence:

> **On March 23, 2026, The 0.5 Chronicles added a continuous 1996–2006 chapter run, extending the project from early connection into normalized digital life.**

### 为什么这一段重要

这一整段补写，填上的是一条历史上非常关键的过渡线。

如果此前第一卷的工作，主要建立的是数字文明尚未完全日常化之前的形成过程，那么这次新增的 1996—2006，则把另一段重要变化写得更清楚了：互联网如何从技术门槛，变成普通人的生活环境。

在这条线里：

- 连接成为经验，
- 收件箱成为数字生活的一间房，
- 搜索成为新的定向能力，
- 网吧完成早期接入的民主化，
- 网页开始获得商业性，
- 记忆开始数字化，
- 即时通讯让在场变得可见，
- 论坛让线上身份变厚，
- 宽带让网络从事件变成背景，
- 信息流重组注意力，
- 用户参与开始帮助共同整理网络。

### 这份说明意味着什么

这是一份项目扩展说明。

它标志着项目的历史线不再停留在此前的闭合点，而开始更清楚地向“互联网成为普通社会基础设施”的年份延伸。

一句记录：

> **2026 年 3 月 23 日，《地球文明0.5编年史》新增 1996—2006 连续章节，项目主线正式从早期连接扩展到数字生活日常化阶段。**
