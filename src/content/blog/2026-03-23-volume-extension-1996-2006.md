## English

Today, **Volume II** of **The 0.5 Chronicles** can be formally marked as having taken shape.

Its title is:

> **The Age of Connection (1996–2010)**

This volume follows the years in which the internet stopped being only a technical threshold and became an ordinary social environment. It entered homes, inboxes, cybercafes, markets, identities, pockets, clouds, and the visible stream of daily life.

### What this volume now contains

The newly established line includes the following years and themes:

- 1996 — connection becomes experience
- 1997 — the inbox becomes a room of digital life
- 1998 — search becomes a new sense
- 1999 — cybercafes democratize access
- 2000 — the web begins to connect value
- 2001 — memory turns digital
- 2002 — presence becomes visible through instant messaging
- 2003 — online identity thickens through forums and usernames
- 2004 — broadband turns access into background
- 2005 — the feed reorganizes attention
- 2006 — user participation helps organize the web
- 2007 — computing moves toward the body
- 2008 — software enters platform distribution
- 2009 — digital life escapes the single machine
- 2010 — daily life enters the stream of continuous publication

### Why this matters

If Volume I recorded the years before digital civilization became normal life, Volume II records the years when digital civilization actually crossed into ordinary life.

This is not merely a continuation of technology. It is the period in which connection, search, communication, identity, storage, distribution, and visibility gradually reorganized daily experience.

The internet was no longer simply a specialized environment. It became a background condition, a social rhythm, and eventually a visible layer of ordinary existence.

### What this formation note means

This note marks a structural change in the project.

1996–2010 is no longer treated as an informal extension attached to earlier material. It is now recognized as a full volume line with its own title, scope, and entry page.

In one sentence:

> **On March 23, 2026, Volume II of The 0.5 Chronicles, *The Age of Connection (1996–2010)*, formally took shape.**

## 中文

今天，《地球文明0.5编年史》**第二卷**可以正式标记为已经成形。

它的卷名是：

> **《连接的年代》（1996—2010）**

这一卷写的是互联网如何不再只是技术门槛，而开始成为普通社会环境。它进入家庭、收件箱、网吧、市场、身份、口袋、云端，也进入了日常生活那条持续可见的时间流。

### 这一卷现在包含什么

目前已经建立起来的主线，包括以下年份与主题：

- 1996 —— 连接成为经验
- 1997 —— 收件箱成为数字生活的一间房
- 1998 —— 搜索成为新的感官
- 1999 —— 网吧完成早期接入的民主化
- 2000 —— 网页开始连接价值
- 2001 —— 记忆开始数字化
- 2002 —— 即时通讯让在场变得可见
- 2003 —— 论坛与用户名让线上身份变厚
- 2004 —— 宽带让接入变成背景
- 2005 —— 信息流开始重组注意力
- 2006 —— 用户参与开始帮助共同整理网络
- 2007 —— 计算开始贴近身体
- 2008 —— 软件进入平台化分发秩序
- 2009 —— 数字生活摆脱单机命运
- 2010 —— 日常生活进入持续发布的时间流

### 为什么这一卷重要

如果说第一卷记录的是数字文明尚未成为日常生活之前的形成过程，那么第二卷记录的，就是数字文明真正进入普通生活内部的过程。

这不只是技术继续发展的一段时间，而是连接、搜索、沟通、身份、存储、分发与可见性，如何一步步重组日常经验的一段历史。

互联网不再只是专业环境，它开始成为一种背景条件、一种社会节奏，最终也成为普通存在方式里可被持续看见的一层表面。

### 这份成形说明意味着什么

这份说明标记的是项目结构上的一次变化。

1996—2010 这一段，不再只是附着在旧材料后面的非正式延伸，而是已经被正式承认为一卷：它有自己的卷名、自己的范围，也有了自己的卷页面入口。

一句记录：

> **2026 年 3 月 23 日，《地球文明0.5编年史》第二卷《连接的年代》（1996—2010）正式成形。**
